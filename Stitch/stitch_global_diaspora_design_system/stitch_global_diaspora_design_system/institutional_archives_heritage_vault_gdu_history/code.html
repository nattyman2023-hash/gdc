THEOLOGICAL TREASURE

The Diaspora Psalter, 1782

"For the memory of our fathers is the light for our children."

This digitized volume represents the only surviving copy of the Diaspora Psalter, smuggled across borders during the great migration. Each page has been scanned at 1200 DPI to preserve the intricate gold-leaf work and the personal notes scribbled in the margins by its various keepers.

University scholars are currently annotating the text for contemporary research into early diaspora liturgical practices.
