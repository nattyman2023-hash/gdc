Heritage & Vision

Our Story

"Rooted in the Great Commission, established for the global diaspora."

Global Diaspora University was born from a divine mandate to provide world-class academic training to leaders within the global movement of peoples. Founded in 1998, our institution has stood as a beacon of Christian scholarship, navigating the complexities of modern displacement with the timeless truth of the Gospel.

We serve those who are far from home, yet close to the heart of God. Our origins trace back to a small prayer circle of academics and missionaries who recognized that the greatest intellectual and spiritual potential often resides within the diaspora. Today, we are a global campus, uniting students across six continents in a rigorous pursuit of truth, beauty, and goodness.

Authority of Scripture expand_more
We believe the Bible, consisting of the Old and New Testaments, to be the only inspired, inerrant, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is the supreme and final authority in all matters of faith and conduct.
The Triune God expand_more
We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. One in essence, yet distinct in personhood and function.
The Person and Work of Jesus Christ expand_more
We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, His bodily resurrection, and His personal return in power and glory.
Kingdom Leadership & Marketplace Ethics expand_more
We believe that every believer is called to be a steward of God's grace in the world. This includes the pursuit of professional excellence and the highest ethical standards in every sphere of influence.
