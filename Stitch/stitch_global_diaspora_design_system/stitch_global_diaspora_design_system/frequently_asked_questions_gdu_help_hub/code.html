General Questions

Global Diaspora University is committed to fostering academic excellence rooted in traditional Christian values. Our mission is to empower students from across the diaspora to become leaders through a curriculum that balances rigorous academic inquiry with spiritual formation.

Yes, GDU is fully accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC) and maintains specific programmatic accreditations for our business, theological, and nursing divisions.

school

Admissions

For the Academic Session 2024, the Early Decision deadline is November 1st, and the Regular Decision deadline is January 15th. International applicants are encouraged to apply by December 1st to allow time for visa processing.

GDU currently operates a test-optional admissions policy. While we welcome scores from students who feel they reflect their academic potential, they are not mandatory for admission.
