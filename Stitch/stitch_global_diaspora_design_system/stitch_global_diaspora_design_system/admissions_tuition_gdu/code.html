Academic Excellence

GDU seeks students who demonstrate strong academic potential and a commitment to global leadership. Applicants must provide high school transcripts or previous degree certificates.

  • verified Minimum 2.8 GPA (or international equivalent)
  • verified Official secondary/post-secondary transcripts
  • verified Personal Statement of Faith & Purpose
